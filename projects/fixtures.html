UTM testing shoulder

A Shoulder to allow a standart UTM machine to perform microtensile testing reliably. To meet the budgetry requirements for my undergraduate thesis, I chose to build micro-tensile samples. I designed shoulders that would extend the micro tensile sample without affecting the gauge length.

Wire cutting fixtures

A Fixture for a wire-arc cutter to fix an AM build plate so that we could take the builds off easily (build plate as seen in my Undergrad Thesis)

Converging Diverging Nozzle

A converging-diverging Nozzle for a Potassiom salt model rocket that I designed and helped build The design was informed by thrust calculations using OpenMotor, dimensions of the cuel chamber, and CDF simulations to omtimize the outlet velocity.

Mill Engine

CAD design of an engine for a Mill I did as part of my 'Computer Aided Design' class.
